Shape & elevation

Tight radii and hairline borders carry the structure. Elevation is exactly three levels — each a drop shadow paired with a backdrop blur and an elevation background fill — used sparingly for menus, drawers and modals.

Corner radius

The spec scale is 0 / 4 / 8 / 12 / full — there is no 16 or 24. (2 and 40 exist as ad-hoc Figma variables only.)

Focus

Two-layer ring: blue outer accent over a contrast inner ring (black on dark, white on light). Thickness scales with element size — no layout shift, ever.

Motion

Quick and functional — fades and short slides, no bounces, no infinite decorative loops. Reduced motion falls back to instant.

Z-index

Values spaced by 100 so intermediate layers can be added without conflicts.
