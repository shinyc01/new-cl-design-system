Components

Search

Two search variants: a compact square field for filtering tables and lists, and a pill-shaped AI smart search for conversational queries. Both support autofill-as-you-type, a filtered suggestion dropdown, and a × clear button when text is present.

Search field

32px square field (radius 4). Autofill-as-you-type with inline ghost completion and filtered dropdown. × clear button appears as soon as text is present. Caret uses --text-accent-cooper amber. Typing state brightens the border to --search-border-typing (40%).

Smart search

Pill-shaped AI search (40px, radius 9999px). Right button toggles between mic (text mode) and keyboard (voice mode) when empty; becomes a filled × as soon as text is present. Same autocomplete dropdown as the search field.
